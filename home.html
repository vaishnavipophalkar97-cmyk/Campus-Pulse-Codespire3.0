Why CampusPulse ?

CampusPulse solves the problem of fragmented campus communication by providing a single, reliable source for all academic, cultural, extracurricular events.
From hackathons and workshops to sports meets and club auditions, CampuPulse simplifies campus life by organizing every event in one place - saving students' time and reducing overload.
Along with these features, CampusPulse also provides a Campus QueryBot which answers all your campus-related queries through generative-ai , Gemini.
